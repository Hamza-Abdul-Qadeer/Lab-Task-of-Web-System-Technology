Introduction:

The Foundation for Advancement of Science and Technology (FAST) is a leading Pakistani private, non-profit educational organization best known for establishing the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, often called FAST University. It plays a key role in advancing computer science, engineering, and management education in Pakistan.
  • Established: 2000
  • Type: Private university
  • Campuses: Islamabad, Lahore, Karachi, Faisalabad, Peshawar
  • Specialization: Computer Science and IT education

Origins and mission

FAST was established in 1980 with the vision of promoting higher education and research in science and technology within Pakistan. It was among the first private initiatives of its kind, designed to complement national efforts in technical education and to help bridge the country’s digital and industrial gap.

Establishment of FAST-NUCES

In 2000, the foundation founded the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (FAST-NUCES), one of Pakistan’s first multi-campus private universities. Its campuses in Islamabad, Lahore, Karachi, Peshawar, and Chiniot-Faisalabad specialize in computer science, electrical engineering, and business administration, producing a large share of Pakistan’s software and IT professionals.

Major Departments

  • Computer Science
  • Software Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Data Science
  • Electrical Engineering

Academic and Research focus

FAST supports rigorous academic programs and applied research, emphasizing innovation and industry collaboration. Its universities are recognized for their demanding curricula and active role in Pakistan’s information technology sector. FAST also hosts conferences and seminars to promote knowledge sharing between academia and industry.

Role in Pakistan’s education landscape

Through FAST-NUCES and related initiatives, the Foundation for Advancement of Science and Technology has helped modernize technical education in Pakistan. Its graduates are prominent in IT entrepreneurship, software development, and research, making FAST a cornerstone institution in the nation’s technological development.
